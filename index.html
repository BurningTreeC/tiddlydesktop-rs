Privacy Policy for TiddlyDesktop-RS

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Introduction

TiddlyDesktop-RS ("we", "our", or "the app") is an open-source application for viewing and editing TiddlyWiki files. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data.

TiddlyDesktop-RS is designed to work entirely offline and locally on your device. The app:

  • Does not collect personal information
  • Does not track your usage
  • Does not use analytics services
  • Does not use advertising
  • Does not transmit any data to external servers

Local Data Storage

TiddlyDesktop-RS stores the following data locally on your device:

  • Wiki Files: Your TiddlyWiki files are stored in locations you choose on your device's storage.
  • Recent Wikis List: A list of recently opened wikis is stored locally to provide quick access.
  • Favicon Cache: Extracted favicon images from your wikis are cached locally for display in the app and home screen widget.
  • Backup Files: If backups are enabled, copies of your wiki files are stored locally.

All this data remains on your device and is never transmitted elsewhere.

Network Access

The app requires the INTERNET permission solely to:

  • Run a local web server for folder-based TiddlyWiki wikis (Node.js server running on localhost)
  • Load local wiki content in the WebView component

No data is sent to external servers. All network activity is strictly local (localhost).

Third-Party Services

TiddlyDesktop-RS does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks.

Children's Privacy

Our app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Open Source

TiddlyDesktop-RS is open-source software. You can review the source code to verify our privacy practices at: https://github.com/BurningTreeC/tiddlydesktop-rs

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact the developer.

TiddlyDesktop-RS is a free and open-source application.
